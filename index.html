PM Surya Ghar:
Muft Bijli Yojana

In order to further sustainable development and people's well-being, we are launching the PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana. This project, with an investment of over Rs. 75,000 crores, aims to light up 1 crore households by providing up to 300 units of free electricity every month.

Consumer Registration Process

Step 01

Download the PM Surya Ghar App using the "Download App" button from the menu. Install the app on your Android device.

Step 02

Open the app. Go to consumer page and select "Apply Now" or click the Login dropdown menu and select "Consumer Login".

Step 03

Enter your valid Registered Consumer Mobile Number, complete the CAPTCHA verification, and check the guidelines checkbox. Then click "Verify".

Step 04

After successful Login, enter your Profile details including Name, Email, Address, State, District, PIN Code and other required details. Click "Proceed Verification".

Step 05

Submit your customer details for processing. Your information will be verified and stored in the system.

Step 06

Complete payment verification by paying Rs. 1.00 (One Rupee). Select your preferred payment method (UPI, Card, or Net Banking) and complete the transaction.

Step 07

Wait for 24 hours to receive confirmation of your application. You will receive an SMS notification once your application is verified and approved.
